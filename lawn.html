Lawn Grass Types

A guide to choosing the right grass for your lawn.

Kentucky Bluegrass

  • Climate: Cool-season
  • Appearance: Rich green, fine texture
  • Best for: Northern climates

Bermuda Grass

  • Climate: Warm-season
  • Appearance: Coarse texture, dark green
  • Best for: Southern climates

Zoysia Grass

  • Climate: Warm-season
  • Appearance: Fine texture, dark green
  • Best for: Warmer regions with partial shade

Fescue Grass

  • Climate: Cool-season
  • Appearance: Fine texture, stays green year-round
  • Best for: Shaded areas

Saint Augustine Grass

  • Climate: Warm-season
  • Appearance: Broad leaf blades, bluish-green
  • Best for: Coastal and tropical regions
