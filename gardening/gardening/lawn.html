Lawn Grass Types

A guide to choosing the right grass for your lawn.

Kentucky Bluegrass

Climate Characteristics

  • Optimal Temperature Range: 60°F to 75°F (15°C to 24°C)
  • Tolerance:
    • Cold Tolerance: Excellent. Can withstand harsh winters and recovers in spring.
    • Heat Tolerance: Moderate. Struggles in prolonged heat without irrigation.
    • Shade Tolerance: Low to Moderate. Prefers full sun but tolerates light shade.
  • Rainfall Requirements: Moderate to high rainfall or supplemental irrigation is needed.

Maintenance Tips for Optimal Growth

  • Water regularly, especially during dry summer spells.
  • Fertilize in fall and spring to support its growth cycles.
  • Overseed periodically to maintain a dense, lush lawn.

Bermuda Grass

Climate Characteristics

  • Optimal Temperature Range: 75°F to 95°F (24°C to 35°C)
  • Preferred Regions: Southern United States, tropical, and subtropical areas.
  • Tolerance:
    • Heat Tolerance: Excellent. Performs well in hot, arid conditions.
    • Cold Tolerance: Moderate. Dormant in winter, recovers in spring.
    • Shade Tolerance: Poor. Prefers full sun (6+ hours of sunlight daily).
  • Rainfall Requirements: Moderate rainfall; drought-tolerant once established.

Maintenance Tips for Optimal Growth

  • Watering: 1–1.5 inches of water per week. Deep, infrequent watering is best.
  • Fertilization: Use nitrogen-rich fertilizer during the growing season.
  • Mowing: Maintain a height of 1–2 inches for a dense lawn.
  • Overseeding: Consider overseeding with ryegrass for year-round greenery in cooler climates.

Zoysia Grass

Climate Characteristics

  • Optimal Temperature Range: 75°F to 90°F (24°C to 32°C)
  • Preferred Regions: Transition zone, southern United States.
  • Tolerance:
    • Heat Tolerance: Excellent. Tolerates high temperatures well.
    • Cold Tolerance: Better than most warm-season grasses; tolerates mild frosts.
    • Shade Tolerance: Moderate. Thrives in full sun but handles partial shade.
  • Rainfall Requirements: Moderate rainfall; drought-tolerant once established.

Maintenance Tips for Optimal Growth

  • Watering: 1 inch of water per week during the growing season; deep, infrequent watering is best.
  • Fertilization: Use a balanced fertilizer in late spring through summer.
  • Mowing: Maintain a height of 1–2 inches for dense, attractive grass.
  • Thatch Control: Aerate or dethatch periodically to prevent thatch buildup.

Fescue Grass

Climate Characteristics

  • Optimal Temperature Range: 60°F to 75°F (15°C to 24°C)
  • Preferred Regions: Cool summers and mild winters, Northern United States.
  • Tolerance:
    • Heat Tolerance: Moderate. Needs adequate water during warm periods.
    • Cold Tolerance: Excellent. Stays green in winter in many areas.
    • Shade Tolerance: High. Great for shaded lawns and landscapes.
  • Rainfall Requirements: Moderate to high rainfall; consistent moisture is necessary.

Maintenance Tips for Optimal Growth

  • Watering: 1–1.5 inches of water weekly; deep, infrequent watering is ideal.
  • Fertilization: Use a nitrogen-rich fertilizer in fall and early spring.
  • Mowing: Keep the grass at a height of 2.5–4 inches for best results.
  • Overseeding: Overseed annually in fall to maintain a lush lawn.

Saint Augustine Grass

Climate Characteristics

  • Optimal Temperature Range: 80°F to 100°F (27°C to 38°C)
  • Preferred Regions: Southern United States, coastal areas.
  • Tolerance:
    • Heat Tolerance: Excellent. Thrives in hot, sunny climates.
    • Cold Tolerance: Moderate. Dormant during colder months but recovers quickly.
    • Shade Tolerance: Good. Great for partially shaded areas.
  • Rainfall Requirements: Requires moderate rainfall; occasional irrigation is needed during dry periods.

Maintenance Tips for Optimal Growth

  • Watering: 1–1.5 inches per week; water deeply for strong roots.
  • Fertilization: Use a balanced fertilizer in spring and summer for better growth.
  • Mowing: Maintain a height of 2.5–4 inches for a lush, green lawn.
  • Pest and Disease Control: Regularly inspect for pests and diseases, and treat accordingly.
