Modern House

Minimalist designs with sleek planters and low-maintenance plants.

  • Use geometric planters for a clean look.
  • Opt for succulents and cacti.
  • Include a small water feature for tranquility.

Cottage Style

Overflowing flower beds and a charming mix of colors.

  • Plant roses, lavender, and daisies for a classic look.
  • Add a wooden bench or arbor.
  • Use winding paths with natural stones.

Traditional Home

Classic landscaping with trimmed hedges and symmetrical layouts.

  • Incorporate boxwood hedges for structure.
  • Use seasonal flowers for color.
  • Maintain a well-manicured lawn.
